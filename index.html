Beyond the Headlines

In the Middle Belt and Northern regions of Nigeria, thousands of believers have lost their homes, their churches, and their lives. This is not just conflict; for many families, it is a systematic erasure of their faith and heritage.

Meet Maryam, a mother of three from Plateau State. When her village was attacked at dawn, she lost everything but her children. Today, she lives in a makeshift camp, relying on the grace of strangers. Your donation provides food, medical care, and secure shelter for survivors like Maryam.
